Game 7 recap: Celtics 109 - Bucks 81. <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Boston won series 4-3 advanced to Eastern Conference Finals to face Miami Heat. The series is over as Celtics made a rain of threes in game 7 they got 22 treys in 55 attempts for 40% while Milwaukee had their worse percentage in 3-pt this playoffs shooting 4-33, 12%. Boston had 88 field goals attempt which of 55 were beyond the arc.<br><br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Grant Williams was the messiah who led Boston to Eastern Conference finals for the 4th time in last 6 years. Batman scored a career-high with 27 points (10-22 fg and 7-18 3pt) 6 rebounds 2 blocks in 39:20 minutes leading the plus/minus among his teammates with +25 as he was the executioner of Lopez, Portis and Buddenholzer system. Their coach decided gift to Williams every open shoot in the way Milwaukee could stop damage that Jaylen Brown and Jayson Tatum was doing.
